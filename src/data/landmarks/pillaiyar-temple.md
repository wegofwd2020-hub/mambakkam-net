No village in Tamil Nadu is without its Pillaiyar shrine. In Mambakkam, the Pillaiyar temple
is central to village affairs — consulted before harvests, ceremonies, and new beginnings.
The deity's presence at the heart of the village connects every household to a shared
moment of intention at the start of whatever matters.
