Ellaiyamman shrines are found at the entrances and borders of villages across Tamil Nadu.
The deity is invoked for the protection of the community, the health of the harvest,
and the safety of those who cross the threshold. Festivals and offerings mark the agricultural
calendar, binding the worship of the goddess to the life of the land.
