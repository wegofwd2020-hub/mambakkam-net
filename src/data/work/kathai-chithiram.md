## What is Kathai Chithiram?

**Kathai Chithiram** (கதை சித்திரம், "story-picture") turns a parent's own story into a short, captioned animation a child with special needs can actually understand — the principle behind _social stories_, made personal and on-demand. The name is stripped before any model, a human reviews every draft, and an offline mode needs no key.

See the [full overview](/kathai-chithiram).
